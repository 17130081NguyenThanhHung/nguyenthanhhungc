From the tiny parakeet to the giant hyacinth macaw, not all parrots place the same demands on their parents. Bird expert and author, Carol Frishmann has written a must-have book for every parrot parent-to-be, an all-in-one guide that will remain indispensable in every bird home, regardless of the size, trainability, or personality of the feathered child.
